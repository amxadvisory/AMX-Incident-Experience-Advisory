The Challenge

In regulated enterprises, major incidents are often technically resolved, yet:

  • Incident bridges feel unstructured and chaotic
  • Communication to leadership and users is inconsistent
  • End-user experience degrades during outages
  • Root causes are poorly governed, leading to repeat incidents
  • Confidence in IT leadership erodes over time

The result: operational risk, reputational exposure, and loss of trust — even when systems come back online.

Our Approach

Structure, clarity, and experience-focused governance for the most critical moments in IT operations.

  • Establish clear Major Incident command models
  • Define who leads, who communicates, and who decides
  • Protect end-user and client experience during outages
  • Strengthen Problem Management and root-cause governance
  • Reduce repeat incidents through governance-led remediation

Tool-agnostic and integrates seamlessly with platforms such as ServiceNow.
